ViSERA

ViSERA is an international freelance project in the field of medical image processing, developed to provide clinical assistance to physicians and enable more precise diagnosis of diseases, especially brain and glandular conditions. This project is led by Dr.Salmanpour, a distinguished specialist in the field of medicine.



Morabaraba [github]

One of the most intriguing applications of AI in our world is within the realm of gaming. I was particularly interested in integrating AI into a game that had not yet been thoroughly explored. Morabaraba, known as one of the most challenging games, posed a unique opportunity to incorporate artificial intelligence. I successfully implemented the Minimax algorithm within this game.
